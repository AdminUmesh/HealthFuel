## What is Zinc?
Zinc is an essential mineral. Your body cannot make it, so you must get it from food or supplements.

## How Zinc Helps the Body
- Supports immunity
- Helps wound healing
- Supports skin health
- Helps hair health if deficiency exists
- Supports testosterone and reproductive health
- Helps taste and smell
- Helps recovery and cell repair

## Daily Need
- Men: 11 mg/day
- Women: 8 mg/day
- Pregnancy: 11 mg/day
- Breastfeeding: 12 mg/day

## Food Sources of Zinc
### Non-Veg
- Eggs
- Chicken
- Fish
- Meat
- Shellfish

### Veg
- Pumpkin seeds
- Nuts
- Chana / Rajma / Dal
- Oats
- Whole grains
- Curd / Milk / Paneer

## Who May Need Extra Zinc?
- Poor diet
- Frequent infections
- Hair fall due to deficiency
- Slow recovery
- Vegetarians with low intake
- Low appetite
